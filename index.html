How long is MBBS in Uzbekistan?

The duration of the MBBS program in Uzbekistan is generally 5 years, which includes both theoretical and practical training. During the program, students learn about various medical subjects such as anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, pathology, microbiology, and clinical medicine. They also receive practical training in hospitals and clinics to gain hands-on experience and develop their clinical skills. After completing the program, graduates receive the degree of Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) and are eligible to practice medicine in Uzbekistan or pursue further specialization in their chosen field.

Cost of MBBS in Uzbekistan

The cost of an MBBS program for Indian students in Uzbekistan may vary depending on the university and the program. However, generally, the tuition fees for the MBBS program in Uzbekistan range from USD 3,000 to USD 6,000 per year. In addition to tuition fees, students may also need to consider other expenses such as accommodation, food, and transportation. The cost of living in Uzbekistan is relatively affordable compared to many other countries, and students can expect to spend around USD 100-150 per month for accommodation and food. Overall, studying MBBS in Uzbekistan can be a cost-effective option for Indian students compared to other countries.
